Land Investments

This is a program aimed at enabling small and medium scale earners acquire land in the most affordable way. The company buys land and sub-divides it into equal portions to all the clients who subscribe and fully pay in this program. This program runs in a cycle also referred to as phases and payments plan are determined by the existing market trends.
Under this program Skylers investment holdings limited has managed to run four successful land investment program phase as follows:

  1. Phase 1 (Gatundia)
  2. Phase 2 (Nanyuki)
  3. Phase 3 (Posta)
  4. Phase 4 (Maundu ni Meri)
  5. Phase 5 (Kisima - on going)


Floating of Shares

There are two types of shares as follows:


1. Equity Shares (also referred to as ordinary shares)

They are one of the most common kinds of shares as it gives the shareholder the right to vote in the company’s meeting and also invest inform of dividends from the profits

2.Preference shares

Are financial instrument owned by companies to review capital that comes with dividend option for shareholders.
